About Us
Welcome to your Emotional Analysis Diary, a cutting-edge web app developed by Marija Nikolić, a student at the University of Zadar, as part of her bachelor's thesis. Our app leverages IBM Watson's Natural Language Processing (NLP) to analyze your diary entries and provide insights into your emotional well-being.

How It Works

Our Emotional Analysis Diary uses IBM Watson's NLP to process your diary entries. You simply write about your day, and Watson's sophisticated algorithms analyze the text to detect and interpret various emotional cues. These insights are then aggregated to provide a comprehensive view of your emotional state on a weekly and monthly basis, helping you track your mental health over time.

Psychology

The emotional analysis is based on principles of affective computing and psychological theories of emotion, particularly the work of psychiatrist Paul Ekman. IBM Watson NLP identifies emotions such as joy, sadness, anger, fear, and disgust within your text entries. This analysis is grounded in psychological research that connects language use with emotional states, allowing the app to provide a nuanced understanding of your emotional landscape.

Bacc Thesis

This project, created for a bachelor's degree thesis by Marija Nikolić, aims to bridge technology and psychology by offering a practical tool for emotional self-awareness and well-being. The thesis explores the effectiveness of using NLP for emotional analysis and concludes that such tools can significantly enhance individuals' understanding of their mental health, promoting proactive management of emotional well-being.
